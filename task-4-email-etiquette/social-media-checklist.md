# 📱 Social Media Responsibility Checklist (For College Students)

## ✅ Do’s

* **Think before you post** – Ensure your content is respectful and appropriate.
* **Protect your privacy** – Use strong passwords and adjust privacy settings.
* **Verify information** – Check facts before sharing news or posts.
* **Be respectful online** – Engage in positive and constructive conversations.
* **Maintain a professional image** – Remember that recruiters may view your profile.

## ❌ Don’ts

* **Don’t share personal information** – Avoid posting sensitive data like address or phone number.
* **Don’t spread misinformation** – Never share unverified or fake news.
* **Don’t engage in cyberbullying** – Avoid harmful comments, trolling, or harassment.
* **Don’t overshare** – Posting too much can affect your digital reputation.
* **Don’t post in anger** – Emotional posts can lead to regret later.

---

💡 *Tip: Your digital footprint lasts forever—use social media wisely!*
