# 🛡️ Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist (For College Students in India)

## ✅ Do's (Safe Practices)

- 🔐 Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts (combine letters, numbers, and symbols).
- 📱 Enable **Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)** on apps like email, banking, and social media.
- 💻 Keep your devices updated with the latest security patches and antivirus software.
- 🔍 Always verify links before clicking, especially from unknown emails or messages.
- 🧾 Use secure websites (look for **https://** and a lock symbol in the browser).
- 👥 Limit sharing personal information (like phone number, address, college details) online.

## 💰 Financial / UPI Safety Tips

- 💳 Never share your **UPI PIN, OTP, or bank details** with anyone—even if they claim to be from the bank.
- 📲 Do not scan unknown QR codes or accept random “collect requests” on apps like Google Pay, PhonePe, or Paytm.

## ❌ Don'ts (Avoid These Mistakes)

- 🚫 Do not use public Wi-Fi for banking or sensitive transactions.
- ⚠️ Avoid downloading apps or files from untrusted sources or links.
- 🧑‍💻 Do not trust fake job offers, scholarships, or giveaways asking for money.
- 🔑 Never save passwords on shared or public computers.
- 📢 Avoid oversharing on social media (like location or travel plans).

## 🚨 Report Cyber Crimes

If you face any cyber fraud or suspicious activity:

- 🌐 **National Cyber Crime Portal:** https://cybercrime.gov.in  
- 📞 **Helpline Number:** 1930  

👉 Report immediately to increase chances of recovering lost money and stopping further fraud.
